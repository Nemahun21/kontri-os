How It Works

Four simple steps to start learning and earning.

Choose your path

Pick a category – History, Krio, Food, Sports, Places, Audio Round – and start at level 1.

Play & Learn

Answer questions in multiple formats. Each level has 3 fixed questions – fair for everyone.

Earn & Progress

Get points for correct answers. Use hints (costs points) or unlock next levels by completing.

Get rewarded

Your total points turn into tokens. Redeem via local telecom networks – your knowledge pays off.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the app free?

Yes, Kontri-Os is completely free to download and play. There are optional in-app rewards that let you redeem points for real value.

How do I redeem points?

Go to your profile in the app and tap the redeem button. Follow the instructions to choose a payment method and submit your request. An admin will process it manually.

What platforms are supported?

Kontri-Os is available for iOS and Android. You can also download the APK directly from this site for Android devices.

Who created Kontri-Os?

The app was built by nemahun21, a developer passionate about Sierra Leonean culture and education.
